My Favorite 80's/90's Sitcoms

  • Frasier
  • Friends
  • Seinfeld
  • The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air

Frasier (1993-2004)

Frasier is about the life of Psychiatrist Dr Frasier Crane, who moves from Boston back to his hometown of Seattle and works as a radio show host at KACL. His segment involves giving advice to callers. The series follows him, his radio producer Roz, his brother Niles, their father, Martin and Martin's caregiver, Daphne. Frasier also navigates his own problems and relationships. Frasier was broadcast on NBC for 11 Seasons.



Friends (1994-2004)

Friends is about the lives of six friends (Chandler Bing, Monica Geller, Rachel Green, Phoebe Buffay, Joey Tribbiani, and Ross Geller). They journey through their twenties and thirties in New York City, spending time together and helping each other get through relationships, career setbacks and more. Friends was broadcast on NBC for 10 Seasons.



Seinfeld (1989-1998)

Seinfeld chronicles the misadventures of stand-up comedian, Jerry Seinfeld and his three friends - childhood friend George Constanza, ex-girlfriend Elaine Benes and eccentric neighbor Cosmo Kramer. Seinfeld and his friends live in New York city, they obsess about the minutiae of life and way too often, wreak havoc on others. Seinfeld was broadcast on NBC for 9 Seasons.



The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air (1990-1996)

Will Smith is a teenager who gets into some trouble in his neighborhood in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His mother sends him to live with his well-to-do Aunt and her family in Bel-Air, California. Will's carefree, loud and boisterous nature clashes with his new home culture but he soon wins over one of his cousins, Ashley. His antics inject much needed life and excitement into the upper class household. The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air was broadcast on NBC for 6 Seasons.
